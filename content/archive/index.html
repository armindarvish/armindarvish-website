Welcome

On this website, you will learn about my professional and recreational activities. Currently, I am an R&D Engineer in bay area, CA. My main area of interest is development/commercializtion of nanosensors especially for molecular sensing and biomedical applications. In the past 12 years, I have been involved in various research and product development efforts. Throughout these years I have gained hands-on skills in various experimental techniques covering biology, chemistry and materials science. I have a broad background in multidisciplinary science and technology, hands-on experience in micro/nanofabrication, optical and electron microscopy, molecular biochemistry, signal and image processing, multiphysics simulations, experimental design and statistical data analysis. I Enjoy working with new technologies and find fast-paced and high-risk, high-reward situations very inspiring.
